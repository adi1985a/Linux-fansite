History of Linus Torvalds

Early Years

Born in Helsinki in 1969. His interest in computers began with the Commodore VIC-20.

Linux Creation

In 1991, as a student, he began working on his own operating system that later became Linux.

Project Growth

Linux quickly became an international project, gathering thousands of programmers from around the world.
