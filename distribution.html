Popular Distributions

Ubuntu

The most popular distribution for beginners. Offers a user-friendly graphical environment and great community support.

Debian

A stable distribution that many other systems are based on, including Ubuntu. Known for reliability and security.

Fedora

An innovative distribution sponsored by Red Hat. Always offers the latest technologies and solutions.

Linux Mint

A user-friendly distribution based on Ubuntu, offering a traditional user interface.
