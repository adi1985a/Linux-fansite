Popular Distributions

Ubuntu

The most popular distribution for beginners. Offers a user-friendly graphical environment and great community support. Perfect for those who are new to Linux or want a stable, well-supported system.

Visit Ubuntu

Debian

A stable distribution that many other systems are based on, including Ubuntu. Known for reliability and security. Ideal for servers and systems where stability is crucial.

Visit Debian

Fedora

An innovative distribution sponsored by Red Hat. Always offers the latest technologies and solutions. Great for developers and those who want to stay on the cutting edge.

Visit Fedora

Linux Mint

A user-friendly distribution based on Ubuntu, offering a traditional user interface. Perfect for Windows users transitioning to Linux, with a familiar desktop environment.

Visit Linux Mint
